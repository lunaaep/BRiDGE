Hello!

Thank you so much for joining BRiDGE!

Our project is called "BRiDGE", to bridge the gap between students and small businesses. We noticed many students on campus wanting to take up a part-time job, but many large chain stores don't offer the flexibility many students need. Additionally, we want to help small businesses gain more traction by connecting with students.

Thus, we created BRiDGE.

Here, students can create profiles that display their unique skills, talents, and interests so they can get jobs that interest them at local businesses.

Local businesses also benefit from BRiDGE as they have the opportunity to hire students who are interested in their company and bring knowledge of new technologies and trends that can help put your business on the map.

We hope you enjoy using BRiDGE!

Thank you,

The BRiDGE Team

"BRiDGE" is a project created for the 2022 DHS Entrepreneurship Challenge
