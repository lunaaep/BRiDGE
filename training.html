How to get a job for teens!

From BRiDGE, bridging the gap between students and local businesses since 2022.


Here's what you'll need to do:

1) Make a great resume. Ask your family, teachers, or any other trusted adults in your life to look over your resume. There are plenty of templates online that will help you with thus process step by step!

2) Start applying. BRiDGE is a great place to start! You will never know if you could've gotten the job unless you apply! There is no harm in applying. Make sure your resume is error free and you put your best foot forward.

3) Be patient. Make sure to be kind and respectful to the hiring managers throughout the process. If they ask you to attend an interview or other type of selection process, say yes! And do your best!

4) Lastly, keep going until you get the job you want!

We hope you enjoy using BRiDGE to achieve your goals. You are never too young to work hard to make your dreams come true!

Thank you,

The BRiDGE Team

"BRiDGE" is a project created for the 2022 DHS Entrepreneurship Challenge
